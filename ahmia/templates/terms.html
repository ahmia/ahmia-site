{% trans "1. What Does Ahmia Do?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Ahmia is a non-commercial, privacy-focused search engine that indexes and displays links to publicly accessible `.onion` services on the Tor network. It provides keyword-based search functionality only. It does not host, upload, or distribute `.onion` content. Ahmia does not offer user accounts, personalization, or any form of interactive services. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Ahmia complies with online safety regulations and laws, including the UK Online Safety Act and the Australian Online Safety Act. Users can report harmful or illegal content. Ahmia actively filters search results and prevents search terms. In cases where users search for CSAM or any sexual content, Ahmia provides links to self-help programs or research surveys instead of showing results. The types of content that Ahmia filters may grow in the future as safety rules change and technology for finding harmful content gets better, all while keeping Ahmia's promise to protect user privacy and anonymity. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Ahmia may conduct user interface experiments, such as A/B tests, to evaluate how effectively help links or alternative search result views reach users, i.e., those seeking harmful content or high-risk terms. These tests may involve offering different layouts, messages, or redirect options on a randomized basis. The goal is to improve user engagement with educational or self-help resources while respecting user privacy. No identification of users is performed during these experiments. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Digital Services Act (DSA) Statement: Ahmia is a small-scale, non-commercial academic project that processes approximately 1,715,358 searches per month, with the total number of unique users being significantly lower. It is designed with privacy-by-design principles and does not collect personal data, use cookies, or store IP addresses; thus, Ahmia cannot estimate the number of users beyond the search count. Most users access the service via the Tor Browser, making it technically impossible to determine their geographic origin or the number of EU-based users. No IP address data is available for the `.onion` version of Ahmia. {% endblocktrans %}
